What is Catan?

Catan is a board game that is played in 3-4 players. Each player has 2 settlements and 2 roads at the start, that they can place on the map to gather resources when the dice are rolled.

Each game, the map is different, as the pieces are placed randomly, face down. Each settlement is considered 1 point, and the first player to get 10 points wins.
