What is Osu! ?

Osu! is a singleplayer rythm game, but it also has a multiplayer gamemode, in which you compete with other players on the same song.

On Osu! you can find almost any song ever written, whether it is an anime intro or a rap song. There are different types of maps for the same song, so the posibilities to play a different style on the same song are almost limitless.

The game is played by mouse/keyboard or even a drawing tablet (which is the most used) and it consists in clicking the right rythm circles at the right time and getting a high accuracy. It sounds simple, but it is pretty difficult.
