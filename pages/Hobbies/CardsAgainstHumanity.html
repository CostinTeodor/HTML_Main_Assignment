What is Cards Against Humanity?

Cards Against Humanity is a board game in which you have to gather points by replying to the card in the middle of the table with the funniest card you have in hand. The one who chooses the funniest card out of all is called the Card Czar and it rotates from player to player.

The cards in this game are also gathered from special card packs that can be(and usually are) not family-friendly.
