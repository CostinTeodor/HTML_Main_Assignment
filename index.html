What are Teo's favourite books?

My favourite books are:

  • Ender's Game
  • George and the secret key of the Universe

A few years ago, I wanted to start reading a book which's title transmits somethning to me. I almost couldn't find anything to confer me this effect, but I found Ender's Game on internet one day. Couldn't find any physical book, so I read it online. With every chapter passed, I fell more in more in love with this book. It had anything I was looking for at that time. It's regarding this genius boy that has been borned because the government ordered so and he's been bullied because he was considered special. Would like to write more about it, but wouldn't like to spoil it.

George and the secret key of the Universe was a book that I started reading because my English teacher asked me to in 7th grade. It was an easy book and informative regarding the universe. Now that I think about it, it was like a cartoon in a book. There was this kid whose parents were anti-technology and he really wanted a laptop and their neighnours were a scientist with his daughter. In the end, he won a contest with a laptop as a reward, with help from his neighbours.

It doesn't seem such interesting, but that's because I missed the best parts in the book. George gets in the universe using Cosmo, a talking computer and there are a lot of adventures that I will let you explore, if you want to read the book.
